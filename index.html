Azumarill changes

  • In Generations 2-5, Azumarill is Water type.
  • In Generations 2-5, Azumarill has a base Special Attack of 50.
  • In Generations 2-4, Azumarill has a base experience yield of 153.
  • In Generation 5, Azumarill has a base experience yield of 185.
  • In Generations 6-7, Azumarill has a base experience yield of 189.
  • In Generations 2-7, Azumarill has a base Friendship value of 70.
